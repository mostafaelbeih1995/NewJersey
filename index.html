ABOUT US

We are proud to reveal that the concept of Arabic Sandals was first introduced by us in the Middle East. Paciotti Arabic sandals have become a status symbol among the elite class of the U.A.E and other Gulf countries. We are on the spot light at some of the top luxury shopping malls in the United Arab Emirates like Dubai Mall, Wafi Mall, Outlet Mall & Marina Mall – Abu Dhabi. Our boutique in Doha at the Land Mark Mall is one of the best performing branch. All of the Middle East operations are controlled by a team of professionals at the head office located in Dubai.

SANDALS

When it comes to luxurined Italian designer Cesare Paciotti is the absolute best choice! In his new line of Arabic-style sandals, Paciotti features an extensive collection of colours and materials all of which proudly displaying the brand’s iconic dagger emblem. The collection features ten unique shoes, all of which are maliciously hand-crafted. When you first put on a pair of his sandals, you will immediately notice the soft, genuine leather that graciously straps across your foot. As you walk, the padded foam heel will allow you to step with grace and comfort. Best of all, you have dozens of options to choose from when selecting your own pair of Cesare Paciotti sandals. From deer and ostrich leather, to textile and cloth, you can pick the best fit for your individual style.
